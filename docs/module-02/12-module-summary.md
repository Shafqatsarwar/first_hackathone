# Module 02 — Final Summary

Digital twins allow humanoid robots to **learn, plan, and interact** safely in virtual environments.  
Gazebo focuses on physics fidelity, Unity on visual realism, and together they prepare AI models for the real world.  
Domain randomization and synthetic data ensure robust perception and control.
